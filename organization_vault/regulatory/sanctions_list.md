**Effective Date:** March 2026
**Document ID:** GFIU-REG-2026-04

## 1. Prohibited Jurisdictions (Blacklist)
Transactions to the following jurisdictions are strictly prohibited. Any attempted transfer must be immediately blocked and a Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) filed.

*   **Republic of Aethelgard:** Classified as a Non-Cooperative Territory. High risk of state-sponsored money laundering and terrorism financing.
*   **Volaria Northern Territory:** Subject to comprehensive economic sanctions (GFIU Order 99-A).
*   **Shadow-Bank Archipelago:** Unregulated offshore sector with zero-transparency banking laws.

## 2. Monitored Jurisdictions (Grey List)
Transactions to these locations require "Enhanced Due Diligence" (EDD) and a Biometric Step-Up challenge.

*   **Cayman Islands:** Enhanced monitoring for tax transparency.
*   **Panama:** Monitored for offshore company formation risks.
*   **Seychelles:** Monitored for international business corporation (IBC) transparency.
